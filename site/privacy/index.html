Morrow 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 22 日 · 开发者：Vanky

简要说明：Morrow 是本地优先的个人记录应用。Vanky 不运营用于接收你的财务记录、梦想、日记或照片的服务器，也不在应用中集成广告、跨应用跟踪或第三方分析 SDK。

1. 适用范围

本政策适用于 Morrow iOS 应用及其官方支持页面，说明应用处理哪些信息、信息保存在何处，以及你可以如何导出或删除这些信息。

2. 保存在设备上的信息

根据你使用的功能，Morrow 可能在你的设备上保存：

  • 账户名称、余额、收入、支出、分类和相关备注；
  • 梦想目标、注资记录、日记正文、标签和日期；
  • 你通过系统照片选择器主动选择的图片；
  • 金额显示、默认账户等应用偏好；
  • 可选 AI 服务的显示名称、Base URL 和模型名称；
  • 你主动保存的 AI API Key。API Key 保存在 iOS Keychain 中，不会显示为明文。

这些信息主要位于应用沙盒和 Keychain 中。Vanky 不会因为你在本地创建或编辑记录而自动收到这些内容。

3. 信息何时可能离开设备

备份、分享与恢复

只有在你主动选择导出或分享备份时，Morrow 才会把经过校验的备份交给 iOS 系统分享或文件界面。你选择的 Files、iCloud Drive 或其他第三方存储服务将按照各自的条款处理该副本。备份可能包含你的账户、交易、梦想、日记、允许的普通偏好和被引用的图片，因此你应像保护其他私人文件一样保护备份。

备份明确排除 AI API Key、Keychain 内容、AI 服务配置、第三方授权、设备标识符、缓存和恢复日志。

照片

Morrow 使用 Apple 提供的系统照片选择器。应用只接收你明确选择的图片，不请求浏览整个照片图库的权限。导入后的图片副本保存在应用沙盒中。

可选第三方 AI 服务

添加 AI 服务配置本身不会把你的记录发送给 Vanky。当前配置和 API Key 保存在设备上。对于任何需要向你选择的第三方 AI 服务发送本地数据的功能，Morrow 会在传输前显示服务来源和数据类别并要求你的明确授权；你可以撤销授权。第三方服务将依据其自身隐私政策处理你主动提交的数据。请勿配置你不信任的服务地址。

联系技术支持

当你主动发送支持邮件时，你的邮箱地址、邮件正文及你选择附加的内容会发送至 Gmail 并用于处理你的请求。请不要在支持邮件中发送 API Key、完整备份文件或不必要的私人记录。

4. 开发者收集、跟踪和广告

Morrow 当前不使用开发者服务器收集应用内记录，不包含广告 SDK，不进行跨应用跟踪，也不包含第三方分析 SDK。官方支持网站是静态页面，不使用 Cookie、分析脚本或广告。

5. 安全措施

Morrow 使用应用沙盒、iOS Keychain、HTTPS AI Endpoint 限制、备份校验和、受限 ZIP 预检及原子恢复机制降低意外泄露或数据损坏风险。任何软件都无法承诺绝对安全；请为设备启用系统密码，并妥善管理你导出的备份和第三方 AI 凭据。

6. 保留、导出与删除

  • 本地信息会保留到你编辑、删除或使用“清除全部数据”为止。
  • 你可以从设置中导出版本化备份，并在确认后用备份替换当前本地数据。
  • “清除全部数据”会删除应用管理的业务记录、媒体、普通设置、AI 服务配置、API Key 和授权。
  • 卸载应用通常会删除应用沙盒，但系统和云端备份行为由 Apple 控制。
  • 你保存到 Files、iCloud Drive 或其他位置的备份不受应用控制，需要由你在对应位置手动删除。
  • 支持邮件会按提供支持、处理争议和履行适用法律义务所需的时间保留。

7. 儿童隐私

Morrow 不是面向儿童设计的服务。由于 Vanky 不通过应用服务器收集应用内记录，因此不会通过应用服务器故意收集儿童的个人信息。如果你认为支持沟通中包含不应保留的儿童信息，请联系我们。

8. 政策更新

如果应用的数据处理方式发生实质变化，本页面会更新生效日期，并同步审查应用内说明和 App Store 隐私信息。建议你在重要版本更新后重新查看本政策。

9. 联系方式

隐私或数据处理问题请联系：koovanky@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Morrow Privacy Policy

Effective: July 22, 2026 · Developer: Vanky

In short: Morrow is a local-first personal record app. Vanky does not operate a server that receives your financial records, dreams, diary entries, or photos, and Morrow contains no advertising, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics SDK.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Morrow iOS app and its official support website. It explains what information the app processes, where it is stored, and how you can export or delete it.

2. Information stored on your device

Depending on the features you use, Morrow may store:

  • Account names, balances, income, expenses, categories, and related notes;
  • Dream goals, funding records, diary content, tags, and dates;
  • Images you explicitly select through the system photo picker;
  • Preferences such as amount visibility and the default account;
  • Display name, Base URL, and model name for an optional AI provider; and
  • An AI API key you choose to save. The key is stored in the iOS Keychain and is not displayed as plaintext.

This information is primarily stored in the app sandbox and Keychain. Vanky does not automatically receive it when you create or edit local records.

3. When information may leave your device

Backup, sharing, and restore

Only when you choose to export or share a backup does Morrow hand a validated backup to the iOS system share or file interface. Files, iCloud Drive, or another storage provider you select processes that copy under its own terms. A backup may contain accounts, transactions, dreams, diary entries, allowed ordinary preferences, and referenced images, so protect it as you would any private file.

Backups explicitly exclude AI API keys, Keychain contents, AI provider configuration, third-party grants, device identifiers, caches, and restore journals.

Photos

Morrow uses Apple's system photo picker. The app receives only images you explicitly select and does not request permission to browse your complete photo library. An imported copy is stored in the app sandbox.

Optional third-party AI services

Adding an AI provider configuration does not send your records to Vanky. Configuration and API keys remain on your device. Before any feature sends local data to a third-party AI service you select, Morrow displays the service origin and data categories and requires your explicit grant; you may revoke that grant. The third party handles submitted data under its own privacy policy. Do not configure an endpoint you do not trust.

Contacting support

If you send a support email, your email address, message, and attachments you choose are delivered through Gmail and used to handle your request. Do not email API keys, complete backup files, or unnecessary private records.

4. Developer collection, tracking, and advertising

Morrow currently does not use a developer server to collect in-app records, contains no advertising SDK, does not perform cross-app tracking, and contains no third-party analytics SDK. The official support website is static and uses no cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising.

5. Security measures

Morrow uses the app sandbox, iOS Keychain, HTTPS AI endpoint restrictions, backup checksums, constrained ZIP preflight, and atomic restore mechanisms to reduce disclosure and corruption risks. No software can guarantee absolute security. Protect your device with a system passcode and safeguard exported backups and third-party AI credentials.

6. Retention, export, and deletion

  • Local information remains until you edit, delete, or use Clear All Data.
  • You may export a versioned backup and, after confirmation, replace local data from a backup.
  • Clear All Data removes app-managed business records, media, ordinary settings, AI provider configuration, API keys, and grants.
  • Uninstalling the app normally removes its sandbox, while system and cloud backup behavior is controlled by Apple.
  • Backups saved to Files, iCloud Drive, or elsewhere remain outside the app and must be deleted by you at that location.
  • Support emails are retained as needed to provide support, resolve disputes, and meet applicable legal obligations.

7. Children's privacy

Morrow is not designed as a service for children. Because Vanky does not collect in-app records through an app server, Vanky does not knowingly collect children's personal information through such a server. Contact us if support communications contain a child's information that should not be retained.

8. Policy changes

If Morrow's data practices materially change, this page will show a revised effective date and the in-app disclosures and App Store privacy information will be reviewed. Please revisit this policy after significant updates.

9. Contact

For privacy or data-processing questions, email koovanky@gmail.com.
